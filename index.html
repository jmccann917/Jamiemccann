When Love Burns Brighter Than the Darkness.

Some love stories are written in fire. Others are carved into skin.

Colton Bennett was born to be a rockstar—tattoos, talent, and a temper the world loved to hate. But behind the stage lights and headlines is a man unraveling quietly. Drowning in expectations. Haunted by pain no one ever sees.

Nikki Thomas was never meant to be visible. Not to the world. Not to him. Hidden behind a pen name and a brutal past, she was just supposed to help manage his life—not rewrite it.

But she was Phoenix long before she was Nikki. And he was hers before he ever knew it.

What began as anonymous letters turns into a connection neither of them are ready for—raw, messy, and addictive. But when secrets come out and danger closes in, love stops being a fairytale. It becomes survival.

And in the fire between them, only one truth remains:

He is the chaos she never saw coming.

She is the quiet he’ll burn the world to keep.

And together, they’re the match the past never saw coming.

Twin Flame is a dark, emotional romance about trauma, obsession, and the kind of love that doesn’t heal you—it brands you. Forever.

About Me

Fueled by coffee, chaos, and a heart that beats for stories that ache, I write romance on the dark side—where love is messy, characters are haunted, and happily-ever-afters are earned through fire. I’m a wife, a mom, and a full-time daydreamer who lives for those quiet moments when books feel like secrets and music says everything I cannot.

Twin Flame was born from a single, haunting moment—a psychic once told me my husband was my twin flame, and that idea never let me go. It sank into my bones, twisted through every shadow I carry, and lit the spark for a story about a love so fierce it destroys and rebuilds. Like the phoenix I’ve always loved, my characters rise from ruin—scarred, stronger, and unrelenting.

My stories are dark, emotional, and tangled with suspense. They’re about resilience, survival, and finding beauty in the broken. About the kind of love that doesn’t run from darkness—but walks straight into it, willingly.

When I’m not writing, I’m tangled in the beautiful chaos of family life, singing way too passionately to songs that understand me, and dreaming up the next story that’ll break your heart—before piecing it back together again.
